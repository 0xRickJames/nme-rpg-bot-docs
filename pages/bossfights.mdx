# Bossfights

Try your luck against the mightiest of the NME Universe.

Fight alone or include other fighters to increase your odds. Word to the wise, the more fighters you have help you take down a Boss, the less you get in Experience and Gold!

## Warrior

<Image
  src="/images/bosses/warrior.png"
  alt="Warrior"
  width={350}
  height={350}
/>

The Warrior has the following stats:

- 300 hit points
- 35 attack
- 27% armor
- Armor:
  - Rare Helmet
  - Rare Boots
- Weapons:
  - Gale Shortsword
  - Tidal Spear
- Spell:
  - None
- Skill:
  - None

Use the command `/warrior` to begin a raid against the Warrior.

You get **100 Experience and 35 Gold**, BUT it's divided by(not between) how many fighter's you included.
